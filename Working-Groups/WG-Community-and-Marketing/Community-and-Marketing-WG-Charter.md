# Community & Marketing Working Group Charter

---

## 1. Purpose

This Working Group exists to grow, engage, and retain the Radix community and manage ecosystem communication and visibility.

---

## 2. Scope

### In Scope

* Community growth and engagement
* Communication and messaging
* Marketing campaigns
* Contributor onboarding

### Out of Scope

* Cannot define DAO strategy
* Cannot commit funds beyond approved budgets

---

## 3. Responsibilities

* Manage communication channels
* Run campaigns and initiatives
* Improve onboarding and contributor experience
* Measure and grow engagement

---

## 4. Deliverables

* Campaign plans
* Content strategy
* Community metrics
* Engagement reports

---

## 5. Leadership Structure

* 2–3 elected Stewards
* Term: 6 months

---

## 6. Steward Responsibilities

* Maintain consistent messaging
* Coordinate campaigns
* Support community contributors
* Track engagement metrics

---

## 7. Decision-Making

* Internal: majority
* DAO approval for major campaigns or budget increases

---

## 8. Budget Authority

* Campaign funding
* Content and community initiatives

---

## 9. Reporting

* Monthly updates
* Engagement dashboard
* Quarterly retrospective

---

## 10. Coordination

* Ecosystem WG (growth alignment and shared initiatives)
* Product WG (technical messaging and launch coordination)
* RAC (compliance and transparency requirements)

---

## 11. Conflicts of Interest

Stewards must:

* Disclose any personal or financial interest in campaigns, vendors, or contributors funded by this WG
* Recuse themselves from decisions where a conflict exists
* Avoid self-dealing in contractor or vendor selection

All disclosures must be public and logged.

---

## 12. Renewal / Sunset

* Standard term: 6 months
* Renewal requires a new DAO governance proposal with updated charter, budget, and performance summary
* If not renewed, all active campaigns must wind down and funds returned to treasury within 21 days
* DAO may dissolve this WG at any time by governance vote
