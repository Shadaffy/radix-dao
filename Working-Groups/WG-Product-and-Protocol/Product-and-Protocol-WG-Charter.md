# Product & Protocol Working Group Charter

---

## 1. Purpose

This Working Group exists to coordinate the development, prioritization, and delivery of the Radix protocol and core product roadmap.

---

## 2. Scope

### In Scope

* Maintain and propose protocol and product roadmaps
* Coordinate development efforts across contributors
* Support delivery of network upgrades (e.g., Xi’an and beyond)
* Identify and prioritize technical improvements

### Out of Scope

* Cannot unilaterally change protocol rules
* Cannot override DAO-approved priorities
* Cannot control treasury outside approved budgets

---

## 3. Responsibilities

* Develop and maintain a public roadmap
* Coordinate contributors and technical teams
* Define and issue RFPs for development work
* Track milestone delivery and performance
* Identify technical risks and dependencies

---

## 4. Deliverables

* Public roadmap
* Quarterly delivery plan
* Technical RFPs
* Progress reports and dashboards

---

## 5. Leadership Structure

* 2–3 elected Stewards
* Term: 6 months
* Elected via DAO governance

---

## 6. Steward Responsibilities

* Maintain roadmap clarity
* Coordinate contributors
* Ensure progress against milestones
* Report to the DAO
* Escalate risks and blockers

---

## 7. Decision-Making

* Internal: simple majority
* Quorum: 2 of 3

DAO approval required for:

* Major roadmap changes
* Budget increases

---

## 8. Budget Authority

* Fund development work within approved budget
* Issue RFPs and allocate resources

---

## 9. Reporting

* Monthly updates
* Quarterly retrospective
* Public dashboard

---

## 10. Coordination

* Ecosystem Growth WG (builders & dApps)
* Governance WG (proposal alignment)
* RAC (compliance)

---

## 11. Renewal / Sunset

* 6-month term
* Renewal via DAO vote
