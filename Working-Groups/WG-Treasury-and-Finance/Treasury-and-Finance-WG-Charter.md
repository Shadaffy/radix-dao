# Treasury & Finance Working Group Charter

---

## 1. Purpose

This Working Group exists to manage, safeguard, and report on the DAO treasury in alignment with community-approved budgets and the DAO's non-distribution principle.

---

## 2. Scope

### In Scope

* Treasury management and multi-signature coordination
* Financial reporting and transparency
* Budget planning and tracking across Working Groups
* Grant administration oversight (milestone tracking, payment authorization)
* Treasury risk and diversification (within DAO-approved policies)
* Compliance with financial reporting obligations

### Out of Scope

* Cannot make unilateral treasury disbursements not authorized by governance
* Cannot define financial strategy independently of DAO-approved priorities
* Cannot override governance decisions on budget allocations
* Cannot invest or diversify treasury assets without an approved Treasury Risk & Diversification Policy and specific governance authorization

---

## 3. Responsibilities

* Maintain public treasury dashboards showing balances, inflows, outflows, and approvals
* Coordinate Authorized Signers for treasury execution within approved proposal limits
* Publish monthly financial updates to the governance forum
* Administer milestone-based payments for approved grants and service contracts
* Flag budget overruns (>10% variance) to the RAC within 10 business days per WG Framework
* Prepare quarterly financial transparency reports per Transparency & Reporting Policy

---

## 4. Deliverables

* Monthly treasury reports (balances, transactions, budget-to-actuals)
* Quarterly financial transparency reports
* Budget tracking dashboard (public, updated monthly)
* Grant milestone payment confirmations
* Annual treasury health summary (prior to Phase 2 transition)

---

## 5. Leadership Structure

* 2–3 elected Stewards
* Term: 6 months
* Elected via DAO governance

---

## 6. Steward Responsibilities

* Maintain accurate and current treasury records
* Coordinate with Authorized Signers on execution timing
* Publish required reports on schedule
* Flag material financial risks to the RAC and community
* Ensure all disbursements have corresponding governance authorization

---

## 7. Decision-Making

* Internal: simple majority of seated Stewards
* Quorum: 2 of 3 (or majority if 2 seated)

DAO approval required for:

* Any disbursement exceeding approved proposal mandates
* Budget carry-forward requests
* Initiating treasury diversification proposals

---

## 8. Budget Authority

* Administer approved Working Group budgets within DAO-authorized limits
* Issue payment instructions to Authorized Signers for milestone-based disbursements
* No independent spending authority — all disbursements must have a corresponding approved governance proposal

---

## 9. Reporting

* Monthly financial updates (balances, transactions, variances) — due by the 7th of each month
* Quarterly retrospective financial report — aligned with Transparency & Reporting Policy schedule
* Public treasury dashboard — updated no less than weekly

---

## 10. Coordination

* Authorized Signers (execution of all treasury transactions)
* RAC (compliance, budget variance escalation, emergency treasury actions)
* All Working Groups (budget administration, milestone payment coordination)
* Governance & Legal WG (financial policy development, compliance alignment)

---

## 11. Conflicts of Interest

Stewards must:

* Disclose any personal, financial, or organizational interest in proposals, grants, or service contracts they administer
* Recuse themselves from payment authorization decisions where a conflict exists
* Avoid self-dealing in contractor selection or grant administration

All disclosures must be public and logged. A Steward with a conflict on a specific disbursement must not be counted toward quorum for that decision.

---

## 12. Renewal / Sunset

* Standard term: 6 months
* Renewal requires a new DAO governance proposal with updated charter, budget, and performance summary
* If not renewed, all pending disbursements must be documented and handed to the RAC for completion or cancellation within 21 days; unspent funds return to treasury
* DAO may dissolve this WG at any time by governance vote; all spending authority ceases immediately
