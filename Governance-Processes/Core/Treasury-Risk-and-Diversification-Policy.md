# Treasury Risk & Diversification Policy

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how the DAO manages its treasury to ensure:

* long-term sustainability
* operational continuity
* protection against native token (XRD) volatility

---

## 2. Guiding Principle

> The treasury must support stable operations regardless of market conditions.

---

## 3. Scope

This policy applies to:

* treasury allocation decisions
* diversification of assets
* maintenance of operational reserves

---

## 4. Stable Reserve (Operational Runway)

---

### 4.1 Reserve Objective

The DAO shall aim to maintain a **Stable Reserve** in low-volatility assets (e.g., USD-pegged stablecoins).

This reserve is intended to:

* protect operational continuity
* reduce dependency on XRD price fluctuations

---

### 4.2 Target Coverage

The Stable Reserve should ideally cover:

* **12 months of approved Working Group operational expenses**

---

### 4.3 Critical Threshold

A **Critical Threshold** is defined as:

* reserve coverage falling below **6 months of operational expenses**

---

### 4.4 Monitoring Responsibility

The Strategic Coordination Working Group is responsible for:

* monitoring treasury runway
* reporting reserve status
* flagging when the Critical Threshold is approached or breached

---

## 5. Diversification Rules

---

### 5.1 Governance Approval

Diversification of XRD into other assets must:

* be proposed through governance
* be approved via a **Treasury Proposal**

---

### 5.2 Execution

Authorized Signers are responsible for executing approved diversification actions.

Execution must:

* follow the approved proposal
* use approved venues and methods
* aim to minimize price impact (slippage)
* consider operational security and liquidity

---

## 6. Risk Considerations

Diversification actions should consider:

* market liquidity
* slippage risk
* counterparty risk
* custody risk

---

## 7. Transparency

All diversification actions must be:

* documented
* reported to the community
* traceable through governance decisions

---

## 8. Relationship to Other Documents

This policy operates alongside:

* Execution & Treasury Actions Policy
* DAO Parameters Registry
* Governance Maintenance & Upgrade Framework

---

## 9. Parameters

Key thresholds and targets may be defined or adjusted in the DAO Parameters Registry, including:

* target reserve duration
* critical threshold level

---

## 10. Amendments

Changes to this policy must follow the Governance Maintenance & Upgrade Framework.

---

## 11. Guiding Principle

> Treasury stability enables governance independence.
