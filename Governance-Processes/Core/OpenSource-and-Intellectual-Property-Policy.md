# Open Source & Intellectual Property (IP) Policy

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy ensures that all work funded or produced by the DAO remains a **public good** for the Radix ecosystem.

It prevents:

* privatization of DAO-funded outputs
* exclusive control by individual contributors or entities

---

## 2. Guiding Principle

> Work funded by the DAO must remain accessible, reusable, and beneficial to the ecosystem.

---

## 3. Scope

This policy applies to:

* software and code
* documentation and written content
* marketing and creative assets
* any deliverables funded through DAO treasury

---

## 4. Default Licensing

---

### 4.1 Software & Code

All software developed:

* by Working Groups
* or funded through DAO proposals (including RFPs)

must be released under a **permissive open-source license**, such as:

* MIT License
* Apache License 2.0

---

### 4.2 Content & Creative Assets

All non-code deliverables, including:

* documentation
* marketing materials
* graphics and media

must be released under:

* **Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY 4.0)**

---

## 5. Ownership & Rights

* The DAO does not grant exclusive ownership of funded outputs
* Contributors may be credited but may not restrict usage
* All outputs must remain freely usable by the ecosystem

---

## 6. Exceptions

Any deviation from default licensing:

* must be explicitly justified
* must be approved via governance proposal

---

## 7. Contributor Agreements

Where applicable:

* contributors may be required to agree to licensing terms
* deliverables must comply with this policy before acceptance

---

## 8. Enforcement

* Non-compliant deliverables may be rejected
* funding may be withheld if licensing requirements are not met
* disputes may be handled through the Dispute Resolution Policy

---

## 9. Transparency

All funded work must:

* clearly state its license
* be publicly accessible
* be linked to its originating proposal

---

## 10. Relationship to Other Documents

This policy operates alongside:

* Working Group Framework
* Execution & Treasury Actions Policy
* Governance Maintenance & Upgrade Framework

---

## 11. Amendments

Changes to this policy must follow the Governance Maintenance & Upgrade Framework.

---

## 12. Guiding Principle

> Public funding creates public goods.
