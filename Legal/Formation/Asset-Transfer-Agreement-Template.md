> **Purpose:** This template governs the transfer of assets — including intellectual property, source code, license rights, and related materials — from any Asset Transferor to Radix DLT DAO LLC. It must be completed and signed before any material asset transfer is executed.
>
> **Usage:** Complete all bracketed fields. Attach the completed IP Schedule as Exhibit A. Both parties sign before the DAO executes the Continuity Statement.

## Article 1 — Definitions

**"Effective Date"** means the date on which both parties have executed this Agreement and the Conditions Precedent in Article 6 have been satisfied.

**"Intellectual Property"** or **"IP"** means all intellectual property rights in or relating to the Transferred Assets, including: copyright, patent rights, trade mark rights, design rights, database rights, trade secrets, and know-how, whether registered or unregistered, and all applications for the foregoing.

**"IP Schedule"** means the schedule of Transferred Assets set out in Exhibit A, completed by the Transferor prior to execution.

**"License Rights"** means any rights granted to the Transferor under a license agreement from a third party that are included in the Transferred Assets and are capable of being sublicensed or assigned to the Company.

**"Source Code"** means the human-readable form of any software included in the Transferred Assets, together with all associated build scripts, configuration files, tests, and documentation.

**"Third-Party Claim"** means any claim, demand, action, or proceeding by a third party arising from or relating to the Transferred Assets, including claims of IP infringement, breach of prior license, or encumbrance.

## Article 2 — Transfer of Assets

### 2.1 Transfer

With effect from the Effective Date, the Transferor hereby transfers, assigns, and conveys to the Company all of the Transferor's right, title, and interest in and to the Transferred Assets listed in Exhibit A, free and clear of all encumbrances except as disclosed in Exhibit A.

### 2.2 License Rights

Where any Transferred Asset comprises License Rights that are not fully assignable without third-party consent, the Transferor shall:

* (a) notify the Company of any such restriction prior to execution
* (b) use reasonable efforts to obtain any required consent before the Effective Date
* (c) grant the Company the broadest sublicense permissible pending consent

### 2.3 Excluded Assets

Any asset not listed in Exhibit A is excluded from this transfer. The Transferor retains all rights to excluded assets.

---

## Article 3 — IP Schedule

The Transferor shall complete Exhibit A (IP Schedule) in full prior to execution. The IP Schedule must list:

* all software repositories included in the transfer, with repository URL and the commit hash or release tag at the date of transfer
* the applicable open-source or proprietary license for each repository
* all trademark registrations or applications included in the transfer, with jurisdiction and registration number
* all patent applications or granted patents included in the transfer, with jurisdiction and application/patent number
* all documentation, whitepapers, and technical specifications included in the transfer
* all third-party License Rights included in the transfer, with the name of the licensor and any restrictions on assignment or sublicensing
* any known encumbrances, liens, or third-party claims against any Transferred Asset

An incomplete IP Schedule is a material defect. The Company may decline to execute this Agreement until Exhibit A is complete and verified.

---

## Article 4 — Representations and Warranties of Transferor

The Transferor represents and warrants to the Company as of the date of execution and as of the Effective Date:

### 4.1 Title and Authority

* The Transferor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Transferred Assets, or holds sufficient rights to transfer them as described in Exhibit A.
* The Transferor has full legal authority to enter into this Agreement and to transfer the Transferred Assets without the consent of any third party, except as disclosed in Exhibit A.
* This Agreement constitutes a valid and binding obligation of the Transferor, enforceable in accordance with its terms.

### 4.2 No Encumbrances

* The Transferred Assets are free and clear of all liens, charges, mortgages, pledges, encumbrances, or security interests, except as disclosed in Exhibit A.
* There are no agreements, commitments, or obligations that would restrict the Company's use, modification, distribution, or sublicensing of the Transferred Assets after the Effective Date, except as disclosed in Exhibit A.

### 4.3 No Infringement

* To the best of the Transferor's knowledge, the Transferred Assets do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.
* The Transferor has not received any written notice alleging that any Transferred Asset infringes the intellectual property rights of a third party.
* There are no pending or threatened Third-Party Claims against any Transferred Asset, except as disclosed in Exhibit A.

### 4.4 License Validity

* All licenses included in the Transferred Assets are valid, subsisting, and in full force and effect.
* The Transferor is not in material breach of any license agreement relating to the Transferred Assets.
* The licenses listed in Exhibit A are the complete and accurate licenses governing each Transferred Asset.

### 4.5 Source Code Completeness

* The Source Code transferred under this Agreement is complete and functional as described in Exhibit A.
* To the best of the Transferor's knowledge, the Source Code does not contain any undisclosed backdoors, malicious code, or known critical security vulnerabilities that have not been disclosed to the Company prior to execution.

### 4.6 Compliance

* The Transferor has complied in all material respects with all applicable laws in connection with the Transferred Assets.
* There are no regulatory holds, governmental restrictions, or sanctions that would prevent or restrict the transfer of the Transferred Assets to the Company.

## Article 5 — Indemnification by Transferor

### 5.1 Transferor Indemnification

The Transferor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company, its Members, Delegates, and governance oversight participants from and against any losses, damages, costs, expenses (including reasonable legal fees), and liabilities arising from:

* (a) any Third-Party Claim that the Transferred Assets are encumbered, subject to prior rights, or infringe the intellectual property of a third party, where such encumbrance or infringement arose prior to the Effective Date
* (b) any breach of the representations and warranties in Article 4
* (c) any failure to disclose a material encumbrance or restriction in Exhibit A

### 5.2 Survival Period

The indemnification obligations in §5.1 survive the Effective Date for a period of **three (3) years**, except that indemnification for claims of fraud or wilful misrepresentation shall survive without time limit.

### 5.3 Indemnification Procedure

To claim indemnification, the Company shall:

* notify the Transferor in writing within 30 days of becoming aware of a Third-Party Claim
* give the Transferor reasonable opportunity to participate in the defense at its own cost
* not settle any Third-Party Claim without the Transferor's prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld)

## Article 6 — Conditions Precedent

This Agreement shall not become effective unless and until all of the following conditions are satisfied:

* (a) the IP Schedule (Exhibit A) has been completed and reviewed by the Company's Legal Signatories
* (b) the Company has adopted a Governance Proposal authorizing this transfer
* (c) the Delegates holding the Treasury Signing function have confirmed that custody systems are operational and ready to receive the Transferred Assets
* (d) KYC verification has been completed for all Delegates holding Delegated Functions
* (e) the Continuity Statement required under Operating Agreement §3.2 has been prepared in agreed form

---

## Article 7 — Acceptance Procedure

### 7.1 Verification

On or before the Effective Date, the Company's Legal Signatories and, where applicable, the Web2 Custodian shall verify that:

* all software repositories listed in Exhibit A have been transferred to Company-controlled access
* all trademark and patent records listed in Exhibit A have been updated to reflect the Company as rights holder or licensee
* the Source Code at the commit hash listed in Exhibit A matches what was transferred

### 7.2 Acceptance Statement

Upon satisfactory verification, the Legal Signatories shall execute an Acceptance Statement confirming that the Transferred Assets have been received in accordance with this Agreement. The Acceptance Statement shall be publicly recorded on the governance forum.

### 7.3 Rejection

If the Company identifies a material discrepancy between the Transferred Assets and Exhibit A, the Legal Signatories may decline to execute the Acceptance Statement and shall notify the Transferor in writing. The parties shall use reasonable efforts to resolve the discrepancy within 30 days. If unresolved, either party may initiate arbitration under Article 8.

---

## Article 8 — Dispute Resolution and Governing Law

### 8.1 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of the Marshall Islands.

### 8.2 Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved in accordance with Operating Agreement §11.1: first by mediation through the Company's Compliance Liaison (30 days), then, if unresolved, by binding arbitration under the International Arbitration Rules of the ICDR, seated in the Republic of the Marshall Islands, conducted in English, by a sole arbitrator.

---

## Article 9 — General Provisions

### 9.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with Exhibit A, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the transfer of the Transferred Assets and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements relating to the same subject matter.

### 9.2 Amendments

This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both parties and, where required, approved by Governance Proposal.

### 9.3 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force and effect.

### 9.4 No Waiver

Failure to exercise any right under this Agreement does not constitute a waiver of that right.
