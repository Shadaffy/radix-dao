# Ecosystem Growth Working Group Charter

---

## 1. Purpose

This Working Group exists to expand the Radix ecosystem through partnerships, developer engagement, and ecosystem initiatives.

---

## 2. Scope

### In Scope

* Partnerships and integrations
* Developer ecosystem growth
* Grants, hackathons, and builder programs
* Exchange and infrastructure relationships

### Out of Scope

* Cannot commit the DAO to partnerships without approval
* Cannot define strategic direction independently

---

## 3. Responsibilities

* Identify and pursue partnerships
* Manage ecosystem grants and programs
* Support dApp and builder growth
* Facilitate integrations

---

## 4. Deliverables

* Partnership pipeline
* Grant programs and outcomes
* Ecosystem metrics (dApps, TVL, activity)
* Reports on integrations

---

## 5. Leadership Structure

* 2–3 elected Stewards
* Term: 6 months

---

## 6. Steward Responsibilities

* Coordinate ecosystem initiatives
* Evaluate partnership opportunities
* Oversee grants and programs
* Report ecosystem growth

---

## 7. Decision-Making

* Internal: majority
* DAO approval required for:

  * Major partnerships
  * Significant funding allocations

---

## 8. Budget Authority

* Allocate grants within approved limits
* Fund ecosystem programs

---

## 9. Reporting

* Monthly updates
* Ecosystem dashboard
* Quarterly review

---

## 10. Coordination

* Protocol & Engineering WG (technical alignment)
* Community WG (activation)
* RAC (compliance)

---

## 11. Renewal / Sunset

* 6-month term
* DAO renewal required
